## SMS Permissions and Financial Message Processing
MFinAgent requests SMS permissions (READ_SMS and RECEIVE_SMS) to provide automatic financial transaction tracking.

What we access:
- SMS messages required to detect mobile-money and banking transaction events.

How we use it:
- Extract transaction details such as amount, date, transaction type, provider, and counterparty.
- Build your in-app transaction feed, summaries, alerts, and financial coaching insights.

What we do not do:
- We do not sell your SMS data.
- We do not use SMS content for advertising profiling.

Control:
- You can deny or revoke SMS permission anytime from Android settings.
- If SMS permission is denied, automatic transaction ingestion may not function.

Retention:
- Parsed transaction records may be retained to provide historical analytics and user-requested features.
